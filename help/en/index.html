Tombola Help

Tombola enables you to play a game of tombola during a video call. Copy the link to the game and share it with other players to hand out the tickets, each generated randomly for them, and optionally a video call link on Skype Meet Now and JItsi Meet as well. Enable screen sharing during the conversation to show the game board to everyone.

Create a game

To play you need to create a game first. There are several ways to do this:

  • From the toolbar: Click on the plus sign in the top left corner of the window

  • From the menu bar: Click on the File menu and then select New Game

  • From the keyboard: Press simultaneously the Shift key, the Command key and the letter N

A dialog box will appear where you can type your desired title, then confirm by clicking on Create.


Invite other players

If you wish to play during a video call, the application assists you by creating a single web link to share both a ticket and a meeting room on Skype Meet Now or Jitsi Meet for you to chat.

  • Open the invite panel: While a game is open, click on the video camera shaped button, right before the window title

  • Choose if and where to generate the meeting: While the invite panel is open, click on one of the following opzions, available on the right section:

    1. Generate meeting on Skype to use Skype Meet Now
    2. Genera meeting on Jitsi to use Jitsi Meet
    3. Don't generate meeting to do just that

  • Copy the web link: While the invite panel is open, click on the Copy button in the left section, or copy the link from the text field directly

  • Invite people via email: While the invite panel is open, click on the Email button in the left section, then send the message via the email client that launches

  • Get a QR code to use as attachment: While the invite panel is open, click on the Save button in the left section, then hit the save button again in the panel that appears

  • Start the video call: While the invite panel is open, click on the Open button down in the left section, then follow the instructions on the video call program that launches

After clicking the link, the player will be shown a web page containing a randomly generated ticket to play with. If a meeting was generated together with the ticket, the player will find a link to it below the game title in the web page.


What you can do during a game

Opening a game you will see the game board, the elapsed time and the remaining numbers to be drawn. Here's what you can do:

  • Draw a number: While a game is open, click on the Draw button at the end of the toolbar. You can also use the menu bar by going to the Game menu and selecting Draw

  • Pause or resume the timer: While a game is open, click on the Pause/Play toggle, right before the Draw button

  • Enable or disable the sound effects: When you draw a number by default it will be repeated out loud; use the Mute/Unmute toggle, before the timer toggle, to change this behaviour

  • Call out the game status: You can request the app to repeat the last drawn number or the elapsed time out loud by clicking on the buttons Repeat and Elapsed Time before the toggles Sound Effects and Timer respectively. You can also find those commands in the Game menu, after the Draw command

After the last number is drawn, the timer stops and a sound effect will be played.


Delete one or all the games

After you finish a game, it's a good idea to delete it to keep the app lightweight and responsive. There are several ways to do this:

  • From the toolbar: While a game is open, click on the trash button right after the game title

  • From the menu bar: While a game is open, click on the File menu and then select Delete Selected. You can also delete all the games at once by selecting Delete All... instead

  • From the keyboard: While a game is open, press simultaneously the Shift key, the Command key and the letter D

  • From the sidebar: Even if no game is open, right click on its name on the list, then select Delete from the menu that appears

In the first three cases a confirmation dialog will appear where you can change your mind by clicking on Cancel.


Change Tombola Preferences

You can tweak the application's behaviour by changing its preferences in the Tombola Preference panel that you can launch by clicking on the gear icon before the window title when you are in the Welcome page. Here's what you can toggle:

  • Synchronization: If the first time you launched the application you were signed in on macOS with your Apple ID, then you were prompted to enable iCloud, which keeps a copy of your games and syncs them across all your devices; here you can enable or disable this feature as you like and check its status.

  • Appearance: Previous versions of the application used a simpler layout; here you can disable the Ticket page, that enables you to quickly generate a ticket for you to play, and the Welcome page, that shows the three latest games you played and to check their progress

  • Game: Here you can find the Sound Effects settings (as seen before) and the Meeting Service preference (that applies on all newly created games)

Please note that these options are stored locally on device and they are not synced even if you enable iCloud
